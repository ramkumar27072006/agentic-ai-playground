# Monthly Challenges

## Challenge Framework

Every month, contributors can tackle a focused engineering or research challenge.

### Challenge Categories

- Agent Engineering
- Tool Use
- Evaluation Systems
- RAG Architectures
- Multi-Agent Collaboration
- AI Safety

## Rewards

- Hall of Fame recognition
- Community visibility
- Research credits

## First Planned Challenge

Build an agent capable of solving a real-world workflow using at least three external tools.
